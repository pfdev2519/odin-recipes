Lentils & Mushroom Bolognese

The perfect comfort meal.

This hearty Vegan Lentils and Mushroom Bolognese is a delicious twist on the classic Italian sauce. Rich in flavor and texture, it combines earthy lentils and savory mushrooms to create a satisfying dish that pairs perfectly with your favorite pasta. Packed with nutrients and protein, this recipe is perfect for a cozy dinner or meal prep!

Ingredients

  • For the Bolognese:
    • 1 cup green or brown lentils, rinsed
    • 1 tablespoon olive oil
    • 1 onion, finely chopped
    • 2 carrots, diced
    • 2 celery stalks, diced
    • 3 cloves garlic, minced
    • 8 oz (225 g) mushrooms, finely chopped (button, cremini, or a mix)
    • 1 can (14 oz) crushed tomatoes
    • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
    • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
    • 1 teaspoon dried basil
    • 1 teaspoon smoked paprika (optional)
    • Salt and pepper to taste
    • 2 cups vegetable broth
    • 1 tablespoon soy sauce or tamari (for gluten-free)
    • 1 bay leaf
  • For the Servings:
    • Pasta of choice (spaghetti, fettuccine, etc.)
    • Fresh basil or parsley, chopped (for garnish)
    • Nutritional yeast or vegan Parmesan (optional)

How to Prepare

  1. Prepare the Lentils
    • In a saucepan, bring 3 cups of water to a boil. Add the rinsed lentils, reduce heat, and simmer for about 20-25 minutes until tender. Drain and set aside.
  2. Sauté the Vegetables
    • In a large skillet or pot, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion, carrots, and celery. Sauté for about 5-7 minutes until softened.
  3. Add Garlic and Mushroom
    • Stir in the minced garlic and chopped mushrooms. Cook for an additional 5-7 minutes, until the mushrooms release their moisture and begin to brown.
  4. Combine Ingredients
    • Add the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, dried oregano, dried basil, smoked paprika (if using), salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  5. Add Broth and Lentils:
    • Pour in the vegetable broth, soy sauce, and add the cooked lentils and bay leaf. Stir to combine.
  6. Simmer
    • Bring the mixture to a gentle simmer. Reduce heat to low and let it cook for about 25-30 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the sauce thickens. Adjust seasoning as needed.
  7. Cook the Pasta
    • While the sauce simmers, cook the pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  8. Serve
    • Remove the bay leaf from the sauce. Serve the bolognese over the cooked pasta. Garnish with fresh basil or parsley, and sprinkle with nutritional yeast or vegan Parmesan if desired.
  9. Enjoy!
    • Dig into this comforting and flavorful vegan dish!

This Vegan Lentils and Mushroom Bolognese is not only delicious but also makes great leftovers and can be easily frozen for future meals. Enjoy!
