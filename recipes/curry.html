Golden Tofu Coconut Curry

Spice Up Your Dinner.

This Golden Tofu Coconut Curry is a vibrant and flavorful dish that combines golden turmeric, creamy coconut milk, and protein-packed tofu.
With a mix of vegetables and aromatic spices, it’s perfect for a cozy meal served over rice or with naan.

Ingredients

  • For the Curry:
    • 1 block (14 oz) firm or extra-firm tofu, pressed and cubed
    • 2 tablespoons coconut oil (or vegetable oil)
    • 1 onion, diced
    • 3 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1 inch fresh ginger, grated
    • 1 bell pepper, chopped
    • 2 carrots, sliced
    • 1 can (14 oz) coconut milk
    • 1 can (14 oz) diced tomatoes (or 2 fresh tomatoes, chopped)
    • 1-2 tablespoons curry powder (adjust to taste)
    • 1 teaspoon ground turmeric
    • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
    • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
    • Salt and pepper to taste
    • 1-2 cups spinach or kale (optional)
    • Fresh cilantro for garnish (optional)
  • For Serving:
    • Cooked rice or quinoa
    • Naan bread (optional)

How to Prepare:

  1. Prepare the Tofu
    • Press the tofu for about 15-20 minutes to remove excess moisture. Cut it into cubes.
  2. Cook the Tofu
    • In a large skillet or pot, heat 1 tablespoon of coconut oil over medium heat. Add the cubed tofu and sauté for about 5-7 minutes, until golden brown on all sides. Remove the tofu from the pan and set aside.
  3. Sauté the Aromatics
    • In the same skillet, add the remaining tablespoon of coconut oil. Add the diced onion and sauté for 3-4 minutes until softened. Stir in the garlic and ginger, cooking for an additional minute until fragrant.
  4. Add Vegetables
    • Add the bell pepper and carrots to the skillet. Sauté for about 5 minutes until they begin to soften.
  5. Add Spices
    • Stir in the curry powder, turmeric, cumin, and coriander. Cook for another minute to toast the spices.
  6. Combine Liquids
    • Add the diced tomatoes and coconut milk to the pan. Mix well, then bring to a gentle simmer.
  7. Simmer and Combine
    • Return the sautéed tofu to the skillet. Let the curry simmer for about 15-20 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld. If using, add spinach or kale in the last few minutes of cooking.
  8. Season
    • Taste and adjust seasoning with salt and pepper as needed.
  9. Serve
    • Serve the curry hot over cooked rice or quinoa, and with naan if desired. Garnish with fresh cilantro.
  10. Enjoy
    • Delight in this warm and comforting Golden Tofu Coconut Curry!

Tips:

  • For an extra smooth texture, you can peel the chickpeas before blending.
  • Feel free to customize by adding roasted red peppers, spices, or herbs for different flavors!

Enjoy your meal!
