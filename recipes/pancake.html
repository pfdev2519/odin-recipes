Banana Pancake

A delicious away to boost your morning.

These fluffy vegan banana pancakes are quick to make and incredibly satisfying. Theyâ€™re naturally sweetened with ripe bananas and can be enjoyed with your favorite toppings like maple syrup, nuts, or fresh fruit.

Ingredients

  • 1 large ripe banana, mashed
  • 1 cup plant-based milk (almond, soy, oat, etc.)
  • 1 tablespoon apple cider vinegar
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour (or whole wheat flour)
  • 2 tablespoons maple syrup (optional)
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Cooking oil (for frying)

How to Prepare:

  1. Prepare the batter
    • In a bowl, mash the ripe banana until smooth.
    • Add the plant-based milk and apple cider vinegar. Mix well to combine.
  2. Mix Dry Ingredients
    • In another bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
  3. Combine
    • Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients, stirring until just combined. If you like, stir in the maple syrup and vanilla extract.
  4. Heat the Pan
    • Heat a non-stick skillet or frying pan over medium heat. Lightly grease it with a small amount of cooking oil.
  5. Cook the Pancake
    • Pour about 1/4 cup of batter onto the skillet for each pancake. Cook for 2-3 minutes, or until bubbles form on the surface and the edges look set.
    • Flip the pancakes and cook for an additional 2-3 minutes, until golden brown and cooked through.
    • Remove the pancakes from the skillet and keep them warm. Repeat with the remaining batter.
  6. Serve
    • Serve the pancakes warm, topped with maple syrup, fresh fruit, nuts, or your favorite toppings.
  7. Enjoy
    • Dig into your delicious vegan banana pancakes!

These pancakes are not only tasty but also nutritious, making them a great way to start your day!
