Classic Hummus

Snacks for afternoon snack cravings!

This creamy and flavorful classic hummus is a staple in Middle Eastern cuisine. Made with simple ingredients, it's perfect for dipping veggies, spreading on sandwiches, or enjoying with pita bread.

Ingredients

  • 1 can (15 oz) chickpeas (garbanzo beans), drained and rinsed
  • ¼ cup tahini
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons lemon juice (freshly squeezed)
  • 1-2 cloves garlic, minced
  • ½ teaspoon ground cumin (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • Water (as needed for desired consistency)
  • Paprika or sumac (for garnish)
  • Fresh parsley (for garnish, optional)

How to Prepare:

  1. Combine Ingredients
    • In a food processor, combine the chickpeas, tahini, olive oil, lemon juice, minced garlic, cumin (if using), and a pinch of salt.
  2. Blend
    • Process the mixture until smooth, scraping down the sides as needed. If the hummus is too thick, add water a tablespoon at a time until you reach your desired consistency.
  3. Taste and Adjust
    • Taste the hummus and adjust seasoning. You can add more salt, lemon juice, or garlic according to your preference.
  4. Serve
    • Transfer the hummus to a serving bowl. Drizzle with additional olive oil, and sprinkle with paprika or sumac and fresh parsley if desired.
  5. Enjoy
    • Serve with pita bread, vegetable sticks, or as a spread in sandwiches.

Tips:

  • For an extra smooth texture, you can peel the chickpeas before blending.
  • Feel free to customize by adding roasted red peppers, spices, or herbs for different flavors!

Enjoy your homemade hummus!
